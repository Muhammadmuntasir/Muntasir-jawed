Smart Arcade

In planning the accommodation spaciousness all around has been the main theme. Availability of ample fresh air and natural light to each and every apartment has been immaculately ensured. All apartments has been designed keeping in mind complete privacy and comfortable living.

Smart Arcade offers you a dream home of your choice comprising 4 and 5 room Super Luxury Apartments, elegantly designed to suit your taste and budget. High standard of finish and use of quality material in all fixtures is to ensure you get the very best you deserve.

Elegant from the outside and beautiful from the inside, Smart Arcade is a project reflecting the taste of class. You will love the flowing layouts, supplemented by quality of fittings and finish that exceed your expectations, ensuring good taste, practicality, elegance and functionality. Round-the-clock fool-proof security monitoring will be provided to ensure total protection. Within the apartments, the visual experience balances drama with harmony. An amazing generosity of space in each apartment is designed for comfortable living. High quality finishes combined with the finest fittings create tastefully crafted rooms.
